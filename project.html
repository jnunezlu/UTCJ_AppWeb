About the project

OrganiGarden 2016

The importance of preserving the environment is, for it is water, food, fuel and raw materials used to manufacture things that are used daily is obtained. In the absence of information on this subject and preventive measures for care is jeopardized human existence. Each person can collaborate in some way, but there needs to be a broadcast medium which provides information to carry out environmental improvement and to promote that culture share in other people and improve the environment. Creating an organic garden involves the conservation of plant diversity, improving the environment and helps to reduce pollution there.

Mision:

Promote, guide and raise awareness of the benefits of the development of organic gardens in the town in order to promote the culture of self sustaining, improving the environment and reducing pollution.

 

Vision:

Achieving that most citizens have more knowledge about the importance of developing and maintaining an organic garden, know its benefits and help conserve the environment.

Project originated by initiative of the UTCJ
